Course Details

Automobile Mechanic

Course Outline

    • 1st Year - Common (Basic theories and practicals of electronic and Telecommunication)
    • 2nd Year - Basic practical skills and theory skills in the relevant job (Inside the institute)
    • 3rd Year - Practical sessions and theories of the production.
    • 4th Year - 4 days per week internship in well recognized industry companies.

Eligibility

  • Passed 6 subjects in G.C.E. O/Ls including Mathematics and Sinhala
  • Have to face a selection test regarding general knowledge,maths,mechanical knowledge and English.

After the Completion

  • Certificate awarded by CGTTI
  • Certificate awarded for NVQ level 4
  • Certificate of NAITA
  • After completing this,you are eligible to follow NVQ level 5 within the same institute.

Most demanded jobs
  • Diesel mechanics
  • Automobile Electrician
  • Motor vehicle body repairer & painter
  • Automobile air conditioning technician


Special note:

Monthly Rs.2000/= is paid by TVC during the study period of the course
